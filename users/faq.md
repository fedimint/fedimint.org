# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I join multiple federations?

Yes. There is no limit to how many federations you can join. Each federation is independent — they have no way of knowing which other federations you're a member of or how much you hold in each.

## Do I need to go through KYC?

No. Fedimint promotes **KYF** (Know Your Friends / Know Your Family) rather than KYC. You should only join federations where you trust the guardians.

## What happens if I lose my phone?

Your wallet app automatically backs up your data (encrypted and sharded) across the federation guardians. You can recover your funds by restoring your wallet — no seed phrase required. The specific recovery process depends on your wallet app and federation.

## Can guardians see my balance or transactions?

No. Fedimint uses Chaumian eCash with blind signatures. Guardians cannot see individual balances, transaction details, or link notes to specific users.

## What if a guardian goes offline?

Fedimint's consensus protocol is designed to tolerate a minority of guardians going offline. The federation continues operating normally. If a majority goes offline, the federation pauses until enough guardians come back online.

## Can I move my Bitcoin out of a federation?

Yes. You can redeem your eCash notes for on-chain Bitcoin, or transfer your funds out via the Lightning Network at any time.

## What is eCash?

eCash notes are digital bearer certificates — think of them as digital banknotes. They're stored on your phone, can be sent to others, and are redeemable for Bitcoin. Unlike account balances, they carry no identity information.
